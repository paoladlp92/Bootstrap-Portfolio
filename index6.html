About Me

Paola de la Peña is a multidisciplinary artist based in Monterrey, Mexico. Her work revolves around the performative, the ephemeral, the material and the feminine entity. Through unraveling concepts and ideas from her experience, the artist, Paola de la Peña, creates artistic pieces that explore a great diversity of disciplines and media, such as visual arts, performance, programming, robotics, and video.
